Have you ever eaten a steak, ice cream or anything that required an utensil? At Sporks R' Us, we believe that evolution is key for mankinds survival, even if that involves kitchen utensils. Customers may shop for various types of sporks online through our service. What is a Spork? It is a fork and a spoon in one.

What is a fork?

  • A utensil with two or more prongs, much like Poseidon's mighty Trident that is used to pick up food

What is a spoon?

  • A utensil used to scoop food most likely named after Dave Matthews Band song "Spoon"

Fork + Spoon = Spork
